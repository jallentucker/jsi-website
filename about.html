About Me

I graduated from the University of Florida in 2013 with a B.S. in Statistics. I am currently receiving training in Portland Code School's Javascript Immersion course in Portland, Oregon, in preparation to enter the software development industry. I previously worked for eight years as a technician specializing in GIS and statistical analysis at an environmental engineering and consulting firm in Gainesville, Florida.

Personal Interests

I spend most of my free time watching and reading about baseball, listening to music, or writing and recording music with friends. I received ten years of private classical piano training as a child and have gone on to play lead guitar in several rock bands. I formerly maintained a blog devoted to the University of Florida men's basketball team.
